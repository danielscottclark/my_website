Research

In this project I designed a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for the Support section of a mobile drone flying application within a team.

I interviewed stakeholders, did user interviews and research, analyzed the data, built a prototype, did usability testing, and iterated on the design with team members.

The product is impressive and the setup can be very technical with a need for support in the field.

The business owners and support technicians said they main desire was to reduce call volume as they anticipated an increase with the new product launch.

Final Findings

After usability testing, it was revealed that the pop up alert were less visible then first thought.

That the menu bar on top was impractical and was better positioned at the bottom of the screen because of how the user interacted with the application when it was docked in the controller.
